User ID insights

Our website analyses and graphically represents Tucson's traffic data collected through various users. We have taken raw data curated by Metropia and analysed them to extract meaningful information such as vehicular speed information, peak traffic hours and the busiest roads of Tucson. Scroll down to view our illustrations of Metropia's data.

Tucson Metro Map

The map below plots all the readings teaken over a month from selected users. It shows some of the busiest routes in Tucson. This data was collected and provided to us by Metropia.

Speed Gauge

The map shows all paths in the Tucson area and the speed of the average driver. The path is color-coded to indicate the driver's speed (eg. 'Red' part along the path mean that the driver was occassionally speeding and 'Yellow' indicates slow moving traffic).

KEY METRICs

  • Congestion

    Our observations indicate that traffic congestion in Tuscon is at its peak from 9 AM to 11 AM

  • Time Spent

    Our estimates are that a average commuter wastes anywhere around 4- 14 hrs a week in traffic


  • Fuel Spent

    Based upon the stationary time and assuming a average fuel efficency of 45 Mpg We estimate that around 1400 gallons of gasoline is wasted each week

  • Grid Locks

    Areas such as Downtown Tuscon are prone to routine congestion and gridlocks . They make up for nearly 20% of the traffic problem metrics used here
